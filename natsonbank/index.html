Helena’s Memorable Dishes
Rating:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

During our exploration of the West Village neighborhood, we stumbled upon Nat's on Bank and were immediately drawn in by its charming interior. The host was jovial and we were fortunate to secure seats at the bar. The bartender was delightful, serving up a superb coffee and offering great wine recommendations. The octopus carpaccio and pear salad were simply spectacular. Overall, Nat's on Bank offers a cozy atmosphere, excellent service, and truly memorable dishes. A must-visit for anyone in the area and don’t miss their funky bathroom.
Taylor’s take on GF
Rating:⭐️⭐️

Key Words: Dine-In, Dinner, $50-100

Great atmosphere but I wouldn’t come here if you’re gluten free. I always check the menu before I go to make sure I can eat, when I showed up what was labeled as gluten free online was not labeled as gf on their menu and they confirmed online was not accurate. I was told fries had cross contamination when in reality they said fries were dipped in beer, that’s fully gluten not cross contamination. I ended up having a burger with no bun which was fine but I wish I was able to get something else. I would encourage them to at least try to have gluten free buns, have some more gf options, and make sure their online menu is accurate.
