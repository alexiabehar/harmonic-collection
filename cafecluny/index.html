Max's Review on Technique
Rating:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

My favorite breakfast menu item so far is the avocado toast with a poached egg and side of greens. The greens were evenly and nicely marinated in a vinegar sauce. The poached egg was a fun and tasty addition to the avocado toast. The avocado spread was good, along with the toast and tomatoes. Overall it was solid but I do tend to prefer more adventurous flavors.

The steel cut oatmeal with fruit was nicely done. The coconut shreds and almond slivers helped it quite a bit and were a nice touch more than regular oatmeal with fruit. I don't think I'll get it again simply because it is huge and I don't want to spike my blood sugar so early in the morning. But some people are eating French toast with syrup for breakfast so for those people this would be an improvement in terms of blood spiking.

Dan's Detailed Account
Rating:⭐️

Key Words: $100+

Cafe Cluny, located at 284 W 12th St, is a quintessential West Village gem that earns its five-star rating with ease. This charming French-American bistro combines excellent food, a cozy ambiance, and attentive service to create an exceptional dining experience.

The menu at Cafe Cluny is impressive and diverse. Whether you’re there for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner, you’ll find a selection of thoughtfully prepared dishes that highlight fresh, high-quality ingredients. The avocado toast and fluffy pancakes are breakfast favorites, while the burger and moules frites stand out on the lunch and dinner menus. Each dish is not only delicious but also beautifully presented.

Cafe Cluny’s vibe is both sophisticated and relaxed, with a quaint, Parisian-inspired decor that makes it an inviting spot for any occasion. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely weekend brunch or a romantic dinner, the warm lighting and tasteful furnishings create a welcoming and intimate atmosphere.

Service at Cafe Cluny is top-notch. The staff are friendly, knowledgeable, and attentive, ensuring that your dining experience is smooth and enjoyable from start to finish.

In summary, Cafe Cluny delivers on all fronts: exquisite food, a delightful ambiance, and impeccable service. It’s a perfect choice for anyone looking to enjoy a memorable meal in one of New York’s most charming neighborhoods. Highly recommended!
Midori's Opinion at Dish Size
Rating:⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Key Words: Dine-In, Dinner, $50-100

Came in for an early dinner during the week with a reservation. Around 5ish it wasn’t too busy. We had an outdoor reservation but switched to inside bc of the heat.

To eat we split the Caesar salad which was tiny. It tasted good, but we only got a couple of bites each. As my main I ordered one of the pasta dishes with shrimp. It was absolutely delicious. The sauce was perfectly light and lemony.

The servers were attentive and kept our drinks full. Overall it’s a cute place and I was satisfied with the experience but I can’t stop thinking about how small the Caesar was.
